Chapter One

The Beginning

✦

Every love story has a beginning.

Ours started the way all the best ones do. Quietly and without warning. With two people who had absolutely no idea what they were walking into.

But something shifted. And neither of us was ever quite the same again.


Chapter Two

A Smile Worth Remembering

🌸

Some moments are too soft to shout about. They live quietly in the chest. Let's revisit a few of them.


I smiled reading that. 🌸
That made my heart do something embarrassing. 💓
Now I need to listen to that immediately. 🎶

Chapter Three

The Things We Never Notice

✦

Love isn't always in the grand gestures. Sometimes it's in the silence that doesn't need to be filled.

It's the way two people learn each other's rhythms. When to speak. When to stay quiet. When a single look says everything words can't hold.

Sixteen months of that kind of knowing is a rare and beautiful thing.


Chapter Four

The Courtroom

⚖️

Court is now in session. You have the right to remain adorable. Answer very carefully.


⚖️ Who starts for doing doing during movie ?

⚖️ Who sends the longer voice notes?

⚖️ Who is more dramatic when they are hungry?

⚖️ Who said "I love you" first, in words or just in the way they acted?

Chapter Five

If Time Could Pause

💞

If you could freeze any moment between us and live inside it forever, which one would you choose?


I would go back there with you in a heartbeat. 💞
Some conversations live rent free forever. 🥹
You know me better than you think. 🌹

Chapter Six

Small Truths

✦
🌙

Love doesn't always announce itself.
Sometimes it arrives in the middle of an ordinary Tuesday
and quietly refuses to leave.

🫀

The heart is a strange thing.
It keeps the most important memories
not in moments, but in feelings it can't quite name.

🕰️

Sixteen months is not a long time.
Sixteen months is also, somehow, a whole world
built from scratch between two people.

🌸

The people who matter most
don't always know the size of the space
they take up in someone else's story.

Chapter Seven

The Laugh Before The Ending

😂

One last game. Did I actually say these things, or did I make them up? Be completely honest.


"You are the only person I would share my last piece of food with. Probably."

"I once wrote you a letter and then never sent it because I got shy."

"I carefully calculated the exact angle of my posture so I would always look effortlessly comfortable near you."

"You make ordinary days feel like something worth remembering."

Final Chapter

One Last Memory

They deserve somewhere beautiful to exist."
